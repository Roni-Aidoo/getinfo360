POLITICS, THE EGDED CENTRE OF THE GHANAIAN LIVES

Jesse Mabery July 16, 2026 2 min read Opinion

Politics, a shining face but a chaotic background. Politics makes people believe is its savior but leaves them full of regrets. Politics is the very thing bringing us down. What say you? Politics is now everywhere, worst... in the very lives of people. The separation of the people due to the numerous point of views involved in Politics, has brought concerns of Unity and Trust being potentially weak among citizens

of Ghana today. There have been concerns about Trust being uncommon and Ghanaians not acting towards the bright future which is sought for the country.

Politics has left Ghanaians puzzled. The citizens of Ghana are left saying "Ghana 3ny3 yie" has undoubtedly proved to be a testament of the people's loss of faith in the country's development. Politics, once seen as a savior in the sight of the people is now seen as the thing making people talk about the unrest of social problems in their local communities.

This local saying which basically means "Ghana will never be a fully developed country", is said among the Youth and the Aged, drilling less patriotism into the minds of Ghanaian citizens.

Politics has somewhat and somehow found its way to be the centre of the very lives of Ghanaians. Ghanaians today attribute every social problem to politics. The talk of Politics among the people and everywhere they go, including workplaces and Ghanaian homes, or even transportations like Trotro and Taxis have had ongoing debates... which has brought concerns of daily arguments among the Ghanaian people. The Unity among the Ghanaian people is now questioned. Is Politics to blame for all of this?
